Me as a Political Activist

I have been interested in politics since my school days and am a dedicated young man who aspires for a better social system. I started party politics systematically with Mr. Dilith Jayaweera in his then Mawbima Janatha Party. In a short time, I was elected as a Youth Executive Committee member. While actively carrying out political activities in that party, I also met and discussed with mature politicians with both similar and contrasting ideologies, always seeking wisdom and advice from them. Below are some of the memorable moments captured with these political leaders.

Dilith Susantha Jayaweera

Founder of Mawbima Janatha Party. Presidential candidate in 2024. Current MP via National List. Media mogul, lawyer & political leader.

Manusha Nanayakkara

Minister of Labour & Foreign Employment (2022). Currently serving as Advisor to the President on Labour & Employment affairs.

Udaya Prabath Gammanpila

Leader of Pivithuru Hela Urumaya. Former Minister of Energy, Parliamentarian for Colombo District, and advocate of nationalist values.

Channa Jayasumana

Former State Minister of Pharmaceuticals & Cabinet Minister of Health during Sri Lanka's reform period post-2022.

Wimal Weerawansa

Leader of the National Freedom Front (NFF). Held cabinet roles in industries, construction, housing, and SME development.

Baththaramulle Seelarathana Thero

Leader of Janasetha Peramuna. Presidential candidate in 2020 & 2024, advocating for "One Country, One Nation, One Law".
